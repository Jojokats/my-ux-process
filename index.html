My Process

Summary Of My UX Process

Research

Figuring out who are our users, what they like, dislike and what problems they are having.

Personas

Creating personas to understand their goals & motivations based on user research.

UI Design

Using the styleguide to add colour to the wireframes & then proceeding to the prototyping phase.

Validating

Learning what your users are doing. Proving your ideas with data, analytics and A/B testing.

Who, What, Where, How?

From discovering users, what they like, dislike, what problems they are having to understanding their motivations and goals. This is the first step followed by creating personas, user stories, sitemap, wireframes, high fidelity mockups and prototyping. This is all done prior to commencing the UI.
